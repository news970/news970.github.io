1. App Description

Compress is a utility application for compressing photos and videos on your iPhone or iPad. Features include single photo compression, single video compression, batch photo compression (up to 50), batch video compression (up to 20), target photo size, and target video size compression with EXIF metadata preservation.

All processing is performed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks. No files are uploaded to any server.

2. Free Tier
