Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

🔒 Health Report PDF does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your health data never leaves your device.

1. Data Not Collected

Health Report PDF is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or medical data of any kind
  • Browsing or search history

The Apple App Store Privacy Label for Health Report PDF is: "Data Not Collected."

2. On-Device Processing

All operations — reading health data, generating PDF reports, exporting CSV files — are performed 100% on your device using Apple's built-in HealthKit and PDFKit frameworks.

Your health data is never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party platform. Health Report PDF does not make any network connections to process your data.

3. HealthKit Data Access

Health Report PDF requests read-only access to Apple Health data solely to generate your PDF reports. The app only reads the specific health metrics you select for export. We never write data back to HealthKit, and we never store your health data outside of the report you generate.

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

4. No Analytics or Tracking SDKs

Health Report PDF does not integrate any analytics frameworks, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. There are no third-party services embedded in the app.

5. No Account Required

Health Report PDF does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. Simply open the app and generate your report.

6. In-App Purchases

Health Report PDF offers optional in-app purchases (subscriptions and a lifetime unlock) through Apple's StoreKit framework. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Verity Tools does not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Health Report PDF does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
