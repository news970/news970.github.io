Frequently Asked Questions

How many tools can I use for free?

You get 10 free operations (lifetime). All 12 tools are available during the free tier. After 10 operations, a Pro subscription is required.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All files are processed 100% on your device using Apple's built-in PDFKit framework. No files are ever uploaded to any server. We don't collect any personal data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select PDF Tools → Cancel Subscription. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open PDF Tools → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Pro access will be restored automatically.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All PDF processing works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed to purchase or restore a subscription.

Which PDF tools are included?

Merge, Split, Compress, Delete Pages, Image to PDF, Rotate Pages, PDF to Images, Lock/Unlock PDF, Reorder Pages, Watermark, Add Page Numbers, and Extract Pages.
